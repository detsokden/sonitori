Community

Engage, contribute, educate

The management and staff at Sonitori's hotel and resort are not only part of the community they live and work in but strive to be positive, supportive members of their community, dedicated to contributing to the sustainable development of Cambodia and its people. We actively encourage our staff to better themselves, their families and engage in humanitarian projects in our community.

Scholarship programs for staff and families

Education is the key to breaking the poverty cycle and improving lives but for many of the Sonitori staff and families, higher education was previously out of reach. We are proud to say that through our scholarship program many of our staff and their children have now been enrolled or reintegrated into school. From kindergarten to university, they are studying subjects such as; law, tourism, business, finance, accounting, IT and English. A few of our team members are even studying for their Masters degrees.

We are very proud of them and their continued efforts to grow!

Environmental Mission

Cambodia is a country with many challenges in the past and even now. A lot of people have hardly the means to survive, electricity is scarce and expensive and even clean water is not available everywhere. We try as a hotel and guest in this country, not to add to the existing problems but to be a part of the solution. That's why we strive to be as clean and as limited as possible in the use of energy and potential pollutions. Together with our staff we strive to make a difference, by:

  • Using organic local fruits and vegetables in our kitchen and bar
  • Recycling our cooking oil to return and transform into bio-diesel for the generator
  • Use accessories made from recycled plastic bags
  • Garbage bags, take-away meal boxes and straws made from compostable sugar cane residue
  • Joining on a regular base the "city clean-up" events
  • Promoting the usage of reusable aluminum water bottles instead of plastic ones which can be refilled for free at the water stations in our hotels
  • Using Solar panels to pre-heat the hot water

We hope you will support our efforts to minimize our impact on this beautiful society.
