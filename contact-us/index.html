About Phnom Penh

Phnom Penh is the capital of Cambodia, located in the middle of the country and is the gateway to all different areas of Cambodia, like the coast in the South, the countryside in the North East, the Angkor region in the West and the river area in the middle.

Sonitori Resort is settled in a quiet area in the centre of the city, close to many interesting area's like the Toul Sleng Museum, many Pagodas, the Russian Market, The independency Monument, restaurants, bars and coffee places.

Phnom Penh is famous for its colonial and Chinese-style, and the world renowed Modern Khmer architectural style of the early 60, the hight of Khmer architecture. Next to the popular temples, royal palace and museum, there are lots of other activities to choose from e.g. visiting the silk Island close to the city, rice-paddy countryside, fishing villages and bird sanctuary near the Mekong.

Access to Phnom Penh is easy. Many international and national airlines have regular flights to Phnom Penh airport, just 20 minutes from the Resort. Bus services from all the countryside area go frequently, or taxi or boat services are available.
