Swimming Pool

Our saltwater pool is the perfect place to unwind after a day of exploring Phnom Penh. Nestled in a lush tropical garden setting, you'll almost forget you're in the heart of the city.

Pool Features:

  • Saltwater system - gentle on skin and eyes
  • Surrounded by tropical gardens
  • Comfortable sun loungers
  • Poolside towel service
  • Open daily from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM
  • Shallow and deep sections for all swimming levels

Whether you want to swim laps or simply float and relax, our pool provides a refreshing escape from the tropical heat.

Restaurant & Bar

Experience a culinary journey at our restaurant, where we offer a unique fusion of Khmer and international cuisine. Our talented chefs use fresh, local ingredients to create memorable dishes.

Dining Options:

  • Breakfast buffet with both Western and Asian selections
  • À la carte lunch and dinner menus
  • Signature cocktails at our bar
  • Happy hour specials (5:00 PM - 7:00 PM)
  • In-room dining available 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM
  • Vegetarian and vegan options available

Join us for a drink at our bar, or enjoy a romantic dinner in our elegant dining area overlooking the pool.

Services

At Sonitori, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional service to make your stay as comfortable and convenient as possible.

Our Services Include:

  • 24-hour front desk assistance
  • Room service from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM
  • Laundry and dry cleaning services
  • Airport transfers (upon request)
  • Tour desk for arranging local excursions
  • Free high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the property
  • Concierge services for restaurant reservations and recommendations
  • Babysitting services (with advance notice)

Whatever you need during your stay, our friendly staff is here to help make it happen.

Spa & Wellness

Our Spa is located on the top floor of the new wing. When you are tired from your day exploring the city, the best way to overcome your fatigue, muscle pain and sore feet is to have a wonderful massage.

Treatments Available:

  • Aromatherapy Oil Massage - Relax your senses with essential oils
  • Japanese Shi-Hatsu Massage - Traditional technique by skilled therapists
  • Foot Massage - Revitalize tired feet after exploration
  • Shoulder & Neck Massage - Targeted relief for tension
  • Traditional Khmer Massage - Ancient healing traditions
  • Nail Treatments - Manicure and pedicure services

Treat yourself well during your stay. You know you deserved it!
